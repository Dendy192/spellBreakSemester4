Spellbuild is a game of magic, adventure, and online multiplayer with battle royale system. If you are new to the world of Spellbuild, here are some information about the game's mechanism and basic knowledge.


1. MANAGE YOUR MANA


Spellbuild is a game of magic and sorceries and you need Mana to perform such magic. Players use magical gauntlets to attack their enemies. Technically, you have unlimited amount of Mana. But you must recharge your mana regularly. You’re more vulnerable while recharging since you can’t cast Spells, and enemies will try to attack you at that moment. Try to time recharges when your character is behind cover or moving quickly.


2. KNOW YOUR GAUNTLETS


You can find gauntlets all around the map. Technically, players can cast an attack Spell without gauntlets, but it’s weaker and has no additional buffs. There are 6 types of gauntlets, all have special attributes and abilities to fight enemies. Gauntlets have five different rarities, from Common to Legendary. Higher rarities do more damage and cost less Mana. This is important because not only does it take less Spells to defeat enemies, you can cast more before recharging.


3. SPELL COMBINATIONS


This game is not only about who is more powerful than the other. In certain situations, the more creative you are, the more advatageous you get. This is because the game's mechanism that allows you to combine multiple spells as your attack. For example, you can combine the element of Toxin and Fire to create burning toxic gas that deals areal damage. Be creative with your combinations!


4. HAVE FUN!


We know that this game can be overwhelming with all the enemies and the vast landscape for you to discover. But in the end, this is just a game, and like any other game, you should have fun and enjoy playing it. Invite your friends into the fun and conquer the world of Spellbuild together!
